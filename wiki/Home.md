# EShipJet Project Wiki

This wiki is the shared knowledge base for EShipJet engineering, QA, deployment, implementation, and support workflows.

Use this wiki to keep project knowledge organized, reviewable, and easy to hand off across development, testing, release, and customer support teams.

## Ownership Model

- Engineering owns setup, architecture, API, and code workflow pages
- QA owns testing, regression, and tester sign-off pages
- Release owners maintain deployment and environment pages
- Support owns troubleshooting, customer workflow, and escalation pages
- Product or implementation owners maintain customer-specific process notes

## Documentation Rules

- Keep Dev, Demo, QAS, customer-specific, and Production details clearly separated
- Do not publish credentials, secrets, customer private data, or internal-only access tokens
- Link to source repositories, pull requests, releases, and runbooks where possible
- Keep customer-impacting behavior documented with date, environment, and owner
- Move temporary investigation notes into permanent pages only after review
